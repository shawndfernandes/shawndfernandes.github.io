Video Project

Project Description

The goal of this project is to create a video file that consists of a blend of atleast two videos with a background music track. The transition between the videos should be with an effect.
I chose to make a video about the seven natural wonders of the world, as mentioned by the below websites.

Seven Natural Wonders
Seven Natural Wonders - Index

I then downloaded different videos from youtube for the seven different natural wonders. I also downloaded a sound track for the video from youtube.
Using SPG-Video Splitter, I extracted only a 5-10 second portion of the video of the several different natural wonders.
I then imported the different videos into windows movie maker, and then added background audio with effects.
I then exported the video with 800x600 resolution, and ensured correct encoder rate to ensure file size just below 10MB with optimal quality.

The detailed steps taken to create the final video file

  1. Download the different videos/ audio from youtube
  2. Open individual video on SPG-video splitter.
  3. Click the Select start and Select end for the video sequence for each video.
  4. Click on Save to save the video.
  5. Import the videos into windows movie maker by clicking Add Videos and Photos button.
  6. Then click on Edit and click on Video Volume and then reduce the volume to zero to remove original volume of the videos
  7. Click on Home then Add Music to add the background music to the video
  8. Click on Animations or Visual Effects to add animations between the video transitions.
  9. Edit the length of the animation by clicking on the Duration button.
  10. Navigate to File, Save Movie, Create Custom Setting to create a custom movie export format.
  11. Move the slider to ensure maximum encoder rate and max file size within the limit, and then click on Save.
